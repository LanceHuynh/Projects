About me

My current status

After having graduated from high school in Vietnam in mid-2016, I proceeded to persuade higher education in Finland, Bachelor of Information Technology to be specific. I am now a second-year undergraduate in Oulu University of Applied Sciences.

Describing myself

To me, life should rather be about enjoying yourself and fulfilling what your heart desires ( for me is being with my family) rather than overpressuring yourself or wasting time worrying about trivial matters because in the end, those things do not matter. I used to have a severe case of procrastinating, I used to curl up like a ball and watch time goes by, knowing the consequences yet do nothing. The anticipation, it left scars in me. Until one day, I told myself I had had enough and swore to never run away from problems.

What I can promise you

Frankly, I am not passionate about coding, I chose it because the university was offering International Business and IT, and I thought the topic of International Business was too vague. My ultimate goal is to enjoy my life with the ones I care about. I am not passionate or ambitous, but I am extremely driven. Neither do I waste time nor slack off, if hard-work is what it takes to achieve my goal, then hard-working I will be, since one can only run away from problems, not the consequences transpire afterwards. If I work, I guarantee you my best, no less.
